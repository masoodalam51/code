Why should I prefer TechElex for project making rather than others?

TechElex is not just a project co-coordinating company. It is a dream of some engineering graduates to do something in life. As we said, you get complete guidance from start to end at TechElex and all the queries are solved. The major deciding factors will also be the cost because you can go and check out the other agents who will charge you more for the same project.

Can I get some description about your projects that we need to choose for our project?

Yes, you can ask any questions you want. Communication is the key to prosper and whatever doubts you’ve, you can contact us freely by mailing us at info@techelex.com. Also, for projects list you can visit our website projects.techelex.com for wide range of Projects.

Do you make projects for all branches of engineering?

As of now, we have just initiated our company. So we only specialize in Electronics, Communication, Industrial Electronics, and Instrumentation. Some projects of electrical and mechanical that involve electronics in their concept can be considered. We can make projects on demand of your choice. Just contact us on our Email ID and we’ll revert back to all your queries.

Which are some areas that you specialize in?

We make projects that are based on instrumentation, electronics, programming on 8051, power electronics, robot-making with different functions, automation systems and all. If you have any projects in mind, you can communicate with us and we’ll make it for you.

Do you make software projects that includes some programming languages like MATLAB, VLSI etc.?

As discussed earlier, we do not commit what we do not specialize in. We believe in quality and we are going to stick by it as we want ourselves to grow. We know our strengths and some areas to work on. Our strengths are core electronic projects revolving around microcontroller 8051 programming, inverters, automation system. So for now, we do not make projects on MATLAB and software oriented projects that are something IT /computer stuff. No false commitments.

When should I pay after selecting a project?

After you had finalized on a project, you need to pay minimum Rs.2000/- as your registration amount. Then you can pay in installments of Rs. 2000. But complete delivery should be done before getting the possession of project.
