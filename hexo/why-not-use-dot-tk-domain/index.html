Why You Don’t Use Dot TK Domain

Below i listed many reason for why you don’t use dot.tk domains.

SEO

SEO[Search Engine Optimization] its key role in any website success. But if you use dot.tk domains for your blog or website search engine website not prioritized your content if you wrote a original content. Any copycat or content thief get higher ranking because a top level domain and your content penalized because its produced on dot.tk domains. With dot.tk domains you can’t get a first position in any search engine and having dot.tk domains affect your branding which is important in blogging or internet world. If you Use dot.tk domains your URL looking short but search engine not cares.

Notes: Write a quality articles and little bit SEO helps you to get best place in search engines.

Advertisement

Now a days main reason for start a blog is making some extra income. These income generate using many ways but a most popular method is apply for Google adsense. Google adsense is a height of advertising and if you able to get account on it then you are lucky. But Google adsense membership for dot.tk domains are like a viewing a dreams with open eyes. Your .tk domain is make things worst and its extremely hard to get Google adsense membership using .tk domains.

If you get another advertiser on your .tk website like chitika then CTR is real concern. Because many user creates a .tk domains only for spamming and hacking stuff so advertiser not fill your ad spot even you get a high quality traffic because you have .tk domains.

Domain Delete

This is a biggest issue if you using .tk domains. If your website is popular like getting a higher rank in Alexa ranking or get a decent PR[Google Page Rank] then you are in danger zone because chances of deletion of your domain is high.
