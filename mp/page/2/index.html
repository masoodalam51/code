Dimmer Through Mobile

This sounds very interesting, isn’t it? Now imagine this scenario, you are lying on your bed lazily and you have that lamp in front of you open. Its light is not letting you sleep. But the switch is very far from your bed. You don’t have the energy to get up to the switch board and dim or turn the lamp off. This is where Dimmer Through Mobile works. The Lamp dimmer through Mobile is a system by which your lamps will be connected to your mobile phones. Now, the mobile becomes the remote of the lamp. As mentioned in the scenario, we will configure the lamps with the GSM of the mobile. Either can be such a way to a particular number, that when it gets a predetermined code of SMS or whatever set possible, the lamp gets turned on or off. Isn’t that great stuff? In the above scenario, you just need to send a code from your configured number to turn lamp ON/OFF. Using this way, you can switch your lamp ON/OFF in your house from anywhere in the world. Even we can come up with different codes for different lamps/LED in your house. So you being in Bangalore, and your house in Mumbai, you can turn your lamp/LED ON/OFF just sending the SMS as it’s GSM-based.

Joystick based wheelchair is by far the most popular type of wheelchair we have in market. Then we have neck-based wheelchairs named as “Automated innovative Wheelchair” which is an exclusive product of TechElex.

Wireless Power Thief Monitoring System

In today’s time, electricity is extremely costly. It is because the demand far surpasses production. Thus there comes a point when people use unfair means for getting electricity. This is nothing but power theft. It not only disturbs the financial system of electricity board, but electricity is wasted and the one who is in real need of electricity such as farmers face lot of problems.
The power company then keeps increasing the unit rates and then more power theft and more rates. This is a cycle which has made electricity very scarce and so we have all this load shedding problems everywhere. Electricity is a boon for mankind, huge industries sustain on electricity. Without it, businesses will come to standstill. So power-theft is grave problem that need to be addressed with precision.
Educating the masses about ill-effects of power theft can surely help in a way or two, but miscreants will continue to do so. So here’s where technology comes to rescue again.
Wireless Power Thief Monitoring System will detect the power theft and warn the supplier about illegal use of power in so and so meter.

Prepaid Automatic Toll Naka

The smart card systems have made the world a much easier place. The prepaid systems have indeed made the world “pay-as-you-use” type of stuff. Now there is no need to run up-to long queues at the toll naka.
The active smart card systems just allow the vehicle to pass off the toll naka if your smart card is recharged. If it’s not, the gate won’t open and you’ll need to recharge your smart card.
This will reduce all the hassles that will come along the way of paying and stopping everytime you pass the toll naka. The active RFID automatically deducts the amount from your card. This is the one of the most beautiful aspects of toll naka that you will pay for the only thing that you will use.
The prepaid automatic toll naka system will save time and help traffic management.
