Central Control Unit For Home Automation

Everyone must be aware of the traditional electric switch board which consists of mechanical switches. But here in our system we are using electronic switches, touch panels and wireless remote control unit. The role of this system is same as that of the old-style switch board, but in a smarter way. You may have seen such automatic systems in high-profile Hollywood movies, and we are creating a sort of similar automatic system.
Want to switch off the fan from comfort of sofa, or dim the far away lights lying in bed, now you can do it with just a press of button. Now magic is in your hands in form of remote control!
The home automation system provides a luxury to your home and it is a type of departure from old style switch boards where you would want to get up now and then for ON/OFF tube lights, fans etc.
With Central Control Unit For Home Automation, everything will be at one place and you will be indeed living in a smart home. We have smartphones, smart TVs, everything is becoming smart, and so this technology makes your home smart. After installation, this will be smart home!
