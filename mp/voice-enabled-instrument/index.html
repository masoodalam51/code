Voice Enabled Instrument

The technology often has make life easier and there are lots of examples to prove the rampant fact. Now there comes another technology that even replaces the ease of pressing a button that keeps it off and your voice is the command. We have often seen this in smart-phones, like SIRI technology that commands the input of your systems, that will obviously add to the fact your voice will give command to ONOFF any instrument.
Now there has been such a generation of voice enabled instrument that will suffice for the manually push button systems that were till now considered as extreme luxury. For example, you need to turn the lights on, you just need to say “LIGHT ON” and it will get switched don automatically. Many devices and instruments can be enacted by this technology.
These systems are usually beneficial for visually impaired personalities. The technology of tomorrow is having a vast scope of array in mere future, and thus this technology is definitely here to stay.
