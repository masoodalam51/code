Tongue Driven Wheel Chair

We have seen many technologically advanced wheelchairs in recent years. The hospital project based on wheelchairs keep on coming day by day. Technology has made life better for handicapped persons as there are so many innovations are being carried out on wheelchairs. Joystick based wheelchair is by far the most popular type of wheelchair we have in market. Then we have neck-based wheelchairs named as “Automated innovative Wheelchair” which is an exclusive product of TechElex.
But have you wondered what do the patients who are paralysed and cannot even move neck with that ease? How can technology help them? The neck-based wheelchair requires some of an effort to turn the neck at a desired angle for the sensors to detect. But there are serious patients who find this neck-movement very painful as they are medically not allowed to move neck.
For this, we have went a step ahead and made a tongue driven wheel chair. Can you imagine that just by movement of the tongue the wheelchair moves?

Wireless Power Thief Monitoring System

In today’s time, electricity is extremely costly. It is because the demand far surpasses production. Thus there comes a point when people use unfair means for getting electricity. This is nothing but power theft. It not only disturbs the financial system of electricity board, but electricity is wasted and the one who is in real need of electricity such as farmers face lot of problems.
The power company then keeps increasing the unit rates and then more power theft and more rates. This is a cycle which has made electricity very scarce and so we have all this load shedding problems everywhere. Electricity is a boon for mankind, huge industries sustain on electricity. Without it, businesses will come to standstill. So power-theft is grave problem that need to be addressed with precision.
Educating the masses about ill-effects of power theft can surely help in a way or two, but miscreants will continue to do so. So here’s where technology comes to rescue again.
Wireless Power Thief Monitoring System will detect the power theft and warn the supplier about illegal use of power in so and so meter.

Hospital Staff Tracking System

Have you ever wondered how nice it will be if you would be able to locate a person without using GPS? The said project does the same. This project is very helpful in closed premises such as hospitals schools etc. You are searching for a particular person nearest to you every person of that institute will be provided active RFID cards which more or less works as a GPS. The person nearest to you will be automatically tracked with the help of RFID provided to him and you can easily locate the person at the centralized server.
This is very useful in hospital where you frequently require hospital staff to attend the patient. With the implementation of this system it becomes easier to search for the nearest nurse, ward boy or a doctor. This in turn can be very crucial as precious time can be saved and attend an emergency faster. This can helpful to lot of patient.
Every RFID will be provided with an exclusive code that will be identified uniquely on the centralized monitoring system. The current location of each member of hospitals will be updated on the centralized monitoring system making the tracking of the person very easy using hospital staff tracking system.
