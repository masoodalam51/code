Prepaid Electricity Bill System

Have you ever wondered how hectic is it to pay your electricity bills? The large sum of your pocket goes to the electricity board every month in form of bill. Always, post-paid always hurts as you feel as if you are been charged anything. You always feel the bill is not fair according to your usage. Well, now as the world is going pre-paid, is it not better to pay for the only much you use. That’s the power of prepaid. Whatever you use, you pay. Thus, prepaid electricity billing system gives a transparency to you as you know for what you are paying for.
It is the most transparent systems in which the case of over-charging after postpaid bills doesn’t occur. It’s the same like your mobile operator, prepaid makes you pay first and then you use accordingly. This might be very effective as those who have installed these prepaid billing systems may get some units in exchange of cash. So the consumer now knows he has this much units remaining and when it gets over, he’ll need to refill it again to consume the electricity. Thus, it may save lot of electricity as the consumer will use electricity less when everything is transparent in front of his eyes. If one person saves a bit electricity due to such systems, just imagine how much electricity can be saved if the system is applied on mass basis. Definitely, we’ll see a lot of electricity saving. Thereby, prepaid electricity bill system may indirectly change the mentality of consumers to save more electricity with on their own and thereby very eco-friendly.
